# Privacy Policy – Discord Bot

**Last Updated:** 11/19/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **n8n-Bot** (“the Bot”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use it. By inviting or using the Bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

The Bot only collects information necessary for its features to function. This may include:

### **1.1 Automatically Collected Data**
- **Server IDs**
- **Channel IDs**
- **User IDs**
- **Role IDs**
- **Message content**, but *only when required for processing commands*.  
  - Message content is **not stored** unless the feature requires persistent storage.

### **1.2 Configuration Data**
Data saved to operate bot settings or server configurations, such as:
- Prefixes
- Feature toggles
- Permissions or allowlists/blocklists
- Custom configuration values

### **1.3 Logging Data (Optional)**
The Bot may temporarily store logs for:
- Error tracking
- Command usage analytics
- Debugging issues

Logs are not used to personally identify users.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
Your data is used solely to:
- Provide and operate bot features
- Maintain server-specific settings
- Improve functionality and stability
- Diagnose issues or errors
- Ensure compliance with Discord’s Terms of Service

The Bot **does NOT**:
- Sell or share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Track or profile users

---

## 3. Data Storage & Retention

### **3.1 Storage**
Some data may be stored in a secure database if required for the Bot's functionality. This includes configuration data or feature-related records.

### **3.2 Retention**
- Temporary data (logs, command context) is cleared regularly.
- Persistent data (server settings, saved features) remains until:
  - You request deletion  
  - The server removes the Bot  
  - The Bot is fully shut down by the developer

---

## 4. Data Sharing

The Bot does **not** share or disclose personal data except:
- If required by law
- If necessary to protect the Bot or its developer(s)
- If needed to comply with Discord’s policies

The Bot does **not** transfer data to external services unless explicitly stated within the bot's features.
